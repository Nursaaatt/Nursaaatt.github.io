James Eugene Carrey born in is -American actor, comedian and artist. Known for his energetic slapstick performances, Carrey first gained recognition in 1990, after landing a role in the American sketch comedy television series In Living Color (1990–1994). He broke out as a star in motion pictures with Ace Ventura: Pet Detective The Mask and Dumb and Dumber (all 1994). This was followed up with Ace Ventura: When Nature Calls Batman Forever (both 1995) and Liar Liar (1997).
